
Change duration of all vanilla stations to 2 hours!
Duration of each station is configurable.

[b]v2.1.0[/b]
- now the mod overwrite the vanilla infinite durations.

[b]v2.0.0[/b]
- added support for Terraria 1.4.4.

Note: In 1.4.4 all stations (except Slice of Cake) has infinite duration. You can still use this mod to get extra duration for Slice of Cake.

GitHub: https://github.com/Zennos/LongerStationsBuffs

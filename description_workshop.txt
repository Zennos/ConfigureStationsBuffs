
Allow you to disable infinite duration and change duration of all vanilla stations!
Also there's a option to persist the buffs after death.

Note: changes will only apply to new buffs, the current buffs will not take effect.

[b]v3.0.0[/b]
- changed buff durations will not be infinite anymore.
- added option to persist buffs after death.

[b]v2.0.0[/b]
- added support for Terraria 1.4.4.

GitHub: https://github.com/Zennos/LongerStationsBuffs
